About Bill Watson

Bill Watson was one of the most generous individuals and he blessed the lives of so many with his generosity, witty humor, smile, and willingness to help anyone. He is missed dearly by so many people that he positively impacted over the years. Bill was an avid golfer and loved being on the golf course. He loved watching live sports and was a passionate Philadelphia Eagles football fan.

He was a treasured friend and family member that we lost too soon. He leaves behind his beloved wife, Andrea and their three beautiful children, Mackenzie, Sydney, and Andrew. He is survived by his mother Ruth, brother Edward (Nicole) and nieces Alayna and Alyssa. Bill was preceded in death by his father Edward.
